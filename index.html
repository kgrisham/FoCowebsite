Innovative Prosthetic Design

Innovative Prosthetic Design caters to children who have lost limbs due to illness, accidents, birth defects, etc. We operate out of England and manufacture prosthetics for children all over the world. We are dedicated to helping families in need and to help children retain their motor functions.
